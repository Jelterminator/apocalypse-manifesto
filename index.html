APOCALYPSE MANIFESTO

How the world will not end

or

The plan for not all starving to death

Max Flow, M.C.
Delft, Netherlands

ABSTRACT: Do not panic!—and slow down. The CO₂ concentration has increased by half over the past 200 years. Temperatures have risen +1.5K and without action they will rise for centuries. We are crossing thresholds that, once crossed, lock us into catastrophic warming. Yet all these problems have solutions, which are feasible provided the vast amounts of labour to be done. If the Egyptians could build the Pyramids then humanity as a whole can save the planet. Do not go gentle into this dark night.
